My approach

Coaching through transitions

I accompany people through transitions – between training and career, between old and new worlds of work, between what was and what is coming. My approach combines career coaching, digital mindset and generational competence into a holistic accompaniment programme.

I'm not a consultant who gives advice – I'm the coach who asks the right questions.

Focus areas

Three coaching fields

Digital Mindset & AI Competence

For students, specialists and leaders who want to understand how AI and automation are changing their world of work – and how to actively shape it rather than react. I help develop an inner attitude that sees disruption as opportunity.

Generational Coaching & Bridge-Building

Between young and experienced, between digital native and old school – I create understanding where there is friction. Whether apprentices and trainers, career starters and established teams: I help both sides to see each other and move forward together.

Career & Personal Development

From finding direction after training to the next career step: I accompany people in finding clarity, recognising their strengths and making courageous decisions.
